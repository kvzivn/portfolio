Grocery Gateway - An online food delivery service

Introduction

Voice technology is quickly seeping into our daily lives, and Grocery Gateway is not late to explore the opportunities that come with it. By collaborating with my UX design program, their UX team knew they would get a lot of insight into how this new technology could impact their business while giving us students a great opportunity to explore the new and exciting world of voice user interfaces. In this project my team and I went through the entire UX lifecycle which involved everything from ideation, research, and the creation of multiple interactive prototypes.

Since VUIâ€™s are a fairly new area in design, we found that there were a lot of interesting opportunities and challenges to explore. The fact that none of us had much experience with voice technologies from before quickly led to a series of interesting questions that we were eager to answer. The collaboration was aimed at exploring the use of voice when managing and ordering groceries online and whether the voice component could enhance or [enhance synonym] the experience in any way.

Our main goal was to help Grocery Gateway get a better understanding of voice user interfaces, what kind of challenges lie ahead, and how they might approach these challenges when designing their own customer facing VUI. A big part of this was creating solutions that would give us insights into what kind of language people would use when managing their online groceries.
