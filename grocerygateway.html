Grocery Gateway - An online food delivery service

Introduction

As part of a joint initiative between Humber College and Grocery Gateway, students took part in a formative user test to determine user pain points and improve the Grocery Gateway app. Students conducted interviews with real users and combined their findings to make educated recommendations that would aim to improve online offerings.

My Role

As part of a joint initiative between Humber College and Grocery Gateway by Longo’s, students took part in a formative user test to determine user pain points and improve the Grocery Gateway app. Students conducted interviews with real users and combined their findings to make educated recommendations that would aim to improve online offerings.

Research Goals

Our goal was to reimagine the “My Favourites” feature in a way that allowed users to shop in an efficient manner while exposing them to new products and moments of inspiration, leading to increased incremental sales and brand loyalty.

  • Increase site efficiency and clarity for users
  • Increase online sales
  • Develop solutions to make My Favourites more intuitive
